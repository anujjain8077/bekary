Simple Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor suger
  • 85 self-raising flour

Notes on Ingredients

Caster Suger
finely graulated white suger.
Self-raising flour
A pre mixed combination of flour and leaevning agents(Usallay salt baking powder).

Method

  1. preheat the oven to 190`C.
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
  3. In a medium bowl,whip together the eggs and castor suger until fluffy.
  4. Fold in flour
  5. pour mixture into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheeated oven,or until the top of the cake springs back when lightly pressed.
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack
